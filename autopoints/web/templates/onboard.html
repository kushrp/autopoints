Welcome to autopoints

autopoints helps you decide when to burn miles vs. pay cash. It pulls cash fares and award availability across major programs, normalizes everything to cents-per-point, and tells you whether a redemption is worth it. This wizard sets up credentials, optional Discord notifications, and saved searches.
